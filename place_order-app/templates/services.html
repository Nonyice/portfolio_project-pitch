Our Services

Indoor and Outdoor Catering

Experience the rich and diverse flavors of Africa with our indoor and outdoor catering services. Our talented chefs are skilled in preparing a wide variety of authentic African dishes that will leave your taste buds tingling with delight. From savory stews and aromatic rice dishes to succulent grilled meats and vibrant vegetarian options, we have something to please every palate. Whether you're hosting a corporate event, wedding, birthday party, or any other special occasion, our catering team will ensure a memorable dining experience for you and your guests.

Confectionaries

Indulge your sweet tooth with our delectable confectioneries. Our expert bakers craft a range of irresistible treats, including cakes, chin-chin, and small chops. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or any other milestone, our custom cakes are baked to perfection and designed according to your preferences. Additionally, our chin-chin and small chops are the perfect bite-sized snacks for any gathering or casual get-together.

Delivery Service

Craving our flavors but can't make it to our restaurant? No worries! We offer a convenient delivery service to bring our delicious dishes right to your doorstep. Simply place an order with us, and our dedicated delivery team will ensure that your food arrives fresh and piping hot. Whether you're ordering for yourself, your family, or a large group, we'll handle it with care and efficiency.
